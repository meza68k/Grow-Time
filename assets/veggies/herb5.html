Sage

Growing Season(s):

Spring through Fall

When to Plant:

Spring, as soon as the danger of the last frost has passed

Sun Requirements:

Full Sun but would prefer partial shade in the heat of the Texas Summer

Frost Tolerant?

Can handle a light frost but nothing below freezing

Time to Harvest:

A couple months after planting, as soon as the leaves are large enough

Feeding:

Fertilize at planting

Potential Problems:

Very few, if any at all
