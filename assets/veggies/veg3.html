Watermelon

Growing Season(s):

Summer through Early Fall

When to Plant:

Late Spring when the weather is consistently warm

Sun Requirements:

Full Sun

Frost Tolerant?

No

Time to Harvest:

75-100 days after planting

Feeding:

Fertilize at planting and again as the plant begins to vine and flower

Potential Problems:

Aphids, Blossom End Rot, Powdery Mildew
