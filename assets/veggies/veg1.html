Tomatillo

Growing Season(s):

Summer through Early Fall

When to Plant:

Late Spring when the weather is consistently warm

Sun Requirements:

Full Sun with lots of water. They are vigourous growing plants

Frost Tolerant?

No

Time to Harvest:

70-90 days after planting, when you see the fruit just start to break through the husk

Feeding:

Fertilize at planting and again after harvesting the first fruit

Potential Problems:

Not many as long as you allow for good air circulation to prevent fungal diseases
