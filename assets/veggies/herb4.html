Lemongrass

Growing Season(s):

Spring, Summer

When to Plant:

Spring, as soon as the danger of the last frost has passed

Sun Requirements:

Full Sun

Frost Tolerant?

No

Time to Harvest:

A few months after planting, as soon as the stalks are about 1/2 inch thick or larger

Feeding:

Feed at planting, it shouldn't need more than that

Potential Problems:

None!
