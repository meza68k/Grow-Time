Don't Forget to Attract Pollinators!

Just as there are bad bugs, there are plenty of beneficial bugs to help defend your crops against pests. They also make sure your plants get pollinated so they can produce the fruit we eat. You need to attract these garden allies with beautiful flowering plants and herbs so be sure to plant some of these in and around your garden.

Some beneficial insects:

  • Bees!
  • Ladybugs
  • Fireflies
  • Green Lacewings
  • Butterflies
  • Nematodes
  • Praying Mantis
  • Spiders
  • Wasps

Here are some plants that will attract beneficial insects to your garden:

  • Sunflowers
  • Lavender
  • Basil (let it flower and bees will go crazy for it!)
  • Ecchinacea (aka Cone Flower)
  • Lantana
  • Daisies
  • Zinnias
  • Marigolds
