Keep an Eye Out for these Pests!

Pests can be abundent in North Texas, especially in the warm season, so it is very important to monitor your plants often for pest activity.

Some common pests:

  • Squash Vine Borer
  • Aphids
  • Cucumber Beetles
  • Squash Bugs
  • Spider Mites
  • Tomato Horn Worm
  • White Flies
  • Grasshoppers
  • Cabbage Loopers

Here are some organic pest treatments:

  • Neem Oil
  • Spinosad
  • Bacillus Thuriengensis (aka BT)
  • Insecticidal Soap
  • Orange Oil
  • Garlic Pepper Spray
