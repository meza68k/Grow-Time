Keep and Eye Out for these Common Diseases!

The best defense against disease is healthy plants and soil but, diseases can sometimes attack even the healthiest of plants. Treatment is usually successful if you catch the disease early enough.

  • Powdery Mildew
  • Black Spot
  • Brown Patch
  • Bacterial Wilt
  • Blight (early or late)
  • Bacterial Leaf Spot
  • Rust
  • Blossom End Rot

Here are some organic disease treaments:

  • Neem Oil
  • Copper Fungicide
  • Sulfur
  • Potassium Bicarbonate
  • Hydrogen Peroxide
  • Horticultural Corn Meal
