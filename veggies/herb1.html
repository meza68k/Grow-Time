Basil

Growing Season(s):

Spring, Summer

When to Plant:

Spring, as soon as the danger of the last frost has passed

Sun Requirements:

Full Sun, Partial Shade

Frost Tolerant?

No

Time to Harvest:

Harvest leaves anytime

Feeding:

Fertilize at planting, it shouldn't need more than that

Potential Problems:

Caterpillars, leaf hoppers, grasshoppers, aphids, spider mites
