Mint

Growing Season(s):

Spring through Fall.

When to Plant:

Anytime

Sun Requirements:

Full Sun, Partial Shade

Frost Tolerant?

Yes. Mint is a perennial...it will die back in hard freezes and return when it warms up.

Time to Harvest:

Anytime

Feeding:

Fertilize at planting, then every Spring

Potential Problems:

Not many. Spider mites, maybe
