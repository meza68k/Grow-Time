Squash

Growing Season(s):

Spring, Summer, Fall

When to Plant:

In Spring, after danger of last frost for Summer harvest or in Late Summer for Fall harvest

Sun Requirements:

Full Sun

Frost Tolerant?

No

Time to Harvest:

50-100 days after planting, depending on the variety

Feeding:

Fertilize at planting and then again after flowers start to form

Potential Problems:

All squash varieties are pretty problematic so they require a lot of attention. Powdery mildew is very common. Pests love this plant, especially the Squash Bug and the Squash Vine Borer. They are both very difficult to control.
