Peppers

Growing Season(s):

Summer through Fall

When to Plant:

Late Spring when the weatehr is consistently warm

Sun Requirements:

Full Sun

Frost Tolerant?

No

Time to Harvest:

60-100 days after planting

Feeding:

Fertilize at planting and then add only a little more when the plants start to set fruit. Peppers don't like much fertilizer.

Potential Problems:

Leaf Miners and Spider Mites
